About Me


Who am I?

My name is Michias Asnake, I am a Front End Developer and Graphic Designer based in MN. I have a A.A. in Graphic Design and will be graduating with a B.A. in Graphic Design in May of 2023.


What do I do?

Most of my current work experience is in print and web design. My expertise lies in combining my graphic desgin knowledge and applying it to web development to enhance user experience and create dynamic designs.


What am I passionate about?

My passions are utilizing web and graphic design to enhance user experience and make fun, responsive website. I enjoy being creative and applying my skills to help individuals and businesses create unique solutions. When it comes to programming and design, I am always excited to learn more so that I can enable myself to always do more and dream bigger.
